🌿 Our Story

From a family dream to a global cocoa brand — discover the roots, mission, and people behind NkiseeCocoa.

Our Mission

🌍 Farm to World, With Purpose

NkiseeCocoa was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that the finest cocoa in the world deserves to reach the finest makers in the world, without compromise. We eliminate every unnecessary link in the chain between our tropical farm and your factory.

We practice regenerative agriculture, invest in our farming communities, and uphold the highest international standards for traceability and sustainability. Every bag of cocoa that leaves our farm carries a promise of quality and integrity.

Company History

Our Journey

Founded
The Beginning — NkiseeFarm Established
A family farming plot in the tropical highlands of Nigeria is dedicated fully to premium cocoa cultivation.
Early Growth
First Export Certification Achieved
NkiseeCocoa receives its first international export certification and completes its inaugural overseas shipment to Europe.
Expansion
Plantation Scale-Up & Rainforest Alliance
