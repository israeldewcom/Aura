Our Story

Rooted in the
Heart of Africa

NkiseeCocoa was founded with a single belief: that the world's best cocoa deserves a world-class platform. We work directly with smallholder farmers, providing fair prices, training, and technology.

1
2017 — Foundation
Started with 12 partner farms in Ashanti, Ghana. Focus on post-harvest quality control and fermentation standards.
2
2019 — Certifications
Achieved UTZ, Rainforest Alliance, and Fair Trade certifications. Expanded to Côte d'Ivoire network.
3
2022 — Digital Platform
Launched buyer portal with real-time inventory, blockchain traceability IDs, and automated quoting engine.
4
2024 — Global Scale
Serving 47 buyers across 12 countries. $47M+ in trade facilitated. 340+ partner farms.
